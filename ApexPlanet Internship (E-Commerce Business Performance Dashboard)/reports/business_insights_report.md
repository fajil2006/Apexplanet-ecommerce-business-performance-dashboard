# Business Insights Report

## Project Overview

This report presents the findings from the Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) performed on the Superstore dataset. The objective is to identify key business trends, sales performance, profit performance, and customer behavior.

## Category Analysis

### Sales Performance by Category

The Technology category generated the highest sales among all product categories.

### Profit Performance by Category

The Technology category also generated the highest profit among all product categories.

### Key Insight

Technology is the best-performing category in terms of both revenue and profitability. This indicates that technology products contribute significantly to overall business growth.

## Region Analysis

### Sales Performance by Region

The West region recorded the highest sales among all regions.

### Profit Performance by Region

The West region also recorded the highest profit among all regions.

### Key Insight

The West region is the strongest market for the business, contributing the highest revenue and profit compared to other regions.


## Product Analysis

### Top-Selling Products

The analysis of product sales revealed that the following products generated the highest revenue.

The highest-selling product was:

* Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier

### Key Insight

The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier generated the highest sales in the dataset, indicating strong customer demand for premium office equipment and technology products.


### Profit Analysis of Products

The analysis of product profitability revealed that the Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier generated the highest profit among all products.

### Key Insight

The Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier is both the highest-selling and most profitable product in the dataset. This suggests that the product has strong market demand while maintaining healthy profit margins.


## Monthly Trend Analysis

### Sales Trend

The highest sales were recorded in November 2017.

- Highest Sales Month: November 2017
- Sales Amount: 118447.825

### Profit Trend

The highest profit was recorded in December 2016.

- Highest Profit Month: December 2016
- Profit Amount: 17885.3093

### Key Insight

The business experiences strong seasonal performance during the year-end period. Sales and profit tend to increase significantly during November and December.

## Discount Analysis

### Impact of Discount on Profit

The analysis shows that profit decreases as discount levels increase.

### Key Findings

- Discounts between 0% and 20% generally maintain positive profits.
- Discounts above 30% often result in negative profits.
- The highest average loss occurred at a 50% discount level.

### Business Insight

Excessive discounting negatively impacts profitability. The business should carefully manage discount strategies to avoid profit losses while maintaining customer demand.
